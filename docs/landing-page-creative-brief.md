## 1. What Is CulinAIre Kitchen?

CulinAIre Kitchen is an AI-powered operations platform built exclusively for chefs, restaurateurs, and culinary professionals. It's not a recipe app. It's not a generic restaurant POS. It's the first platform that puts AI into the hands of the people who actually run kitchens — combining culinary knowledge, recipe development, inventory management, purchasing, waste reduction, and menu engineering into one system that speaks chef.

**One-liner:** The AI-powered brain for your kitchen — from mise en place to P&L.

**Domain:** www.culinaire.kitchen

---

## 2. Who Is This For?

**Primary audience:** Working chefs, head chefs, sous chefs, kitchen managers, and restaurant operators who run one or more kitchen locations.

**Key traits:**
- They are NOT tech-savvy. They are hands-on professionals who spend 12+ hours on their feet.
- They are time-poor and impatient. If something takes more than 3 taps, they won't use it.
- They are passionate about craft but drowning in operational overhead — stock counts, ordering, waste tracking, food cost calculations.
- They communicate in kitchen language: "mise en place", "par level", "86'd", "on the pass", "FIFO".
- They use phones more than laptops. The back dock, the walk-in cooler, the pass — that's where they work.
- They trust systems that respect their expertise. They don't want to be "taught" — they want a tool that keeps up with them.

**Secondary audience:** Restaurant group operators / HQ managers who oversee multiple locations and need cross-location visibility into stock, purchasing, and food costs.

## 4. The Product — Feature Modules

### AI Culinary Assistant
A conversational AI that actually understands cooking. Ask it about searing techniques, troubleshoot a broken emulsion, explore ingredient substitutions, or learn pastry fundamentals. Every response is personalised to the chef's skill level, equipment, cuisine identity, and restaurant context. This is not ChatGPT with a food prompt — it's a purpose-built culinary knowledge engine.

### Recipe Lab
AI-powered recipe development and refinement. Generate recipes across any cuisine, refine them iteratively with AI, adjust for portions and dietary restrictions, and export with full costing. Includes editorial content generation for menus, social media, and print.

### Patisserie Lab
Specialised pastry and baking module. Understands ratios, hydration, fermentation, tempering, and pastry-specific techniques that general AI tools get wrong.

### Spirits Lab
Cocktail and spirits knowledge module. Cocktail structures, classic recipes, flavour pairing, and spirits fundamentals for bars and restaurants with beverage programs.

### Menu Intelligence
Menu engineering analytics. Upload your menu items with costs and sales data. The system calculates food cost percentages, contribution margins, menu mix, and classifies every dish (Star, Puzzle, Workhorse, Dog). Identify what to promote, reprice, or remove.

### Kitchen Copilot
Daily prep planning. Select which dishes you're prepping for, and the system generates a prioritised task list based on cross-ingredient usage, prep times, and menu classification. Batch common ingredients. Track completion.

### Waste Intelligence
Track every waste event — spoilage, overproduction, trim, plate waste. The system calculates cost impact, identifies patterns, and uses AI to suggest reuse opportunities. "You wasted 4kg of carrot trim this week — here are 3 ways to use it."

### Stock Room (Inventory)
Real-time stock tracking with FIFO batch management. Par levels, stock takes with parallel category counting, inter-location transfers, consumption logging, and AI-powered depletion forecasting. Multi-location with cross-location visibility for HQ.

### Purchasing
Full purchase order lifecycle. Draft POs from par-level suggestions or previous orders. Spend threshold routing — small orders go direct, large orders require HQ approval. PDF generation and supplier email. Mobile-first delivery receiving checklist with discrepancy logging (short delivery, rejections with photo evidence, price variances, substitutions). Credit note tracking. Supplier performance visibility.

### The Bench (Community)
Real-time chat for culinary professionals. Public channels and direct messages. Share recipes as interactive cards. See who's "on the pass" with live presence. A professional kitchen community, not a social media feed.

### Kitchen Shelf
Public recipe gallery. Share your recipes, browse what other chefs are creating, save to your personal shelf. Featured recipes curated by the platform.

## 5. Brand Identity

### Personality
CulinAIre Kitchen is the **quiet confidence of a well-run kitchen**. It's not loud. It's not gimmicky. It's the sous chef who has everything prepped before service starts — competent, reliable, and one step ahead.

### Tone
- Professional but not corporate
- Warm but not soft
- Direct — chefs don't have time for marketing fluff
- Respectful of craft — never condescending about culinary knowledge
- Confident — this is the best tool for the job, and it knows it

### Visual Identity

**Colour palette:**
| Role | Colour | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Primary accent | Warm gold | `#D4A574` |
| Accent hover | Deep gold | `#C4956A` |
| Background | True black | `#0A0A0A` |
| Surface 1 | Dark charcoal | `#161616` |
| Surface 2 | Charcoal | `#1E1E1E` |
| Border | Subtle gray | `#2A2A2A` |
| Text primary | White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Text secondary | Warm gray | `#999999` |
| Text muted | Dark gray | `#666666` |
| Success | Emerald | `#34D399` |
| Warning | Amber | `#FBBF24` |
| Error | Red | `#F87171` |

**Design language:**
- **Dark theme only** — kitchens are dark environments. Bright screens at midnight are hostile.
- **Glass morphism** — backdrop blur, semi-transparent surfaces, layered depth
- **Gold accents** — warm, luxurious, evokes brass kitchen equipment and Michelin-star elegance
- **Subtle glow effects** — amber glow on active elements, not neon or garish
- **Micro-animations** — elements that respond to touch: lift on hover, spring on selection, shimmer on loading
- **No flat surfaces** — every card, panel, and section has depth through gradients, borders, or shadows
- **Typography** — clean, readable, professional. No playful/quirky fonts. This is a professional tool.

**Logo:** The CulinAIre Kitchen wordmark exists. The "AI" in "CulinAIre" is the clever bit — artificial intelligence embedded in the word "culinaire" (French for culinary).

## 7. Technical Notes for the Creative Director

- The landing page will live at `www.culinaire.kitchen` (root route)
- The app itself lives at `www.culinaire.kitchen/chat`, `/inventory`, `/purchasing`, etc.
- The landing page should be a separate, static-ish page — not behind authentication
- It must be fast (no heavy JS frameworks for the landing page itself)
- Dark theme is non-negotiable — it must match the app aesthetic
- Mobile-first responsive design is critical (60%+ of chef traffic will be mobile)
- The gold (`#D4A574`) on black (`#0A0A0A`) palette is the brand — do not introduce new primary colours
- Glass morphism effects (backdrop-blur, semi-transparent cards) should be used sparingly on the landing page for performance
- App screenshots should be real product shots, not mockups — the app is beautiful enough to show as-is
